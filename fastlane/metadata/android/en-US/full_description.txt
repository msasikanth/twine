Take back control of your news. No algorithms. No ads. Just your feeds.

Tired of social media and news apps deciding what you see? Meet Twine, a modern, open-source RSS reader designed to put you back in the driver’s seat. Twine combines a beautiful, distraction-free interface with powerful tools to help you stay informed on your own terms.

Why Twine?

✨ Beautifully Adaptive UI:

Experience a reader that feels alive. Twine’s dynamic theming engine adjusts the app's ambient colors based on the content you’re reading, creating a truly immersive experience. Fully supports Light, Dark, and AMOLED modes.

🚀 Smart & Simple Feed Discovery:

Don’t hunt for RSS links. Simply enter a website homepage URL, and Twine’s smart fetching engine will find the feed for you.

📖 Distraction-Free Reading:

Read articles the way they were meant to be read. Use the "Article Shortcut" to fetch the full text of any post directly in the reader view, bypassing cluttered websites and paywalls.

🔒 Privacy First, Always: 

Your reading habits are your business. Twine has zero ads, no tracking, and collects no personal data. It’s just you and your news.

Power Features for Feed Pros:

* Universal Support: Works with RSS, Atom, RDF, and JSON feeds.
* Stay Organised: Group, pin, and manage your feeds with ease. Use the customizable bottom bar for quick access to your favorite categories.
* Filter the Noise: Use the "Blocked Words" feature to automatically hide posts containing specific keywords you want to avoid.
* Seamless Syncing: Keep your feeds in sync with support for FreshRSS (GReader), Miniflux, and Dropbox (Alpha).
* Import & Export: Moving from another reader? Import your existing feeds via OPML in seconds.
* Search & Bookmark: Find specific posts across all your feeds or save them to your bookmarks to read later.
* Home Screen Widgets: Stay updated at a glance with beautiful widgets that show your latest unread posts (Available via Twine Premium).

Open Source & Community Driven:

Twine is built with love using Kotlin and Compose Multiplatform. It is an open-source project, meaning the community helps keep it transparent, secure, and constantly improving.
