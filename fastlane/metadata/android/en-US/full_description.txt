<p><i>Twine</i> is a multiplatform RSS reader app built using Kotlin and Compose Multiplatform. It features an nice user interface and experience to browse through the feeds, and supports Material 3 content based dynamic theming.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Supports RSS &amp; Atom feeds</li><li>Gorgeous home feed</li><li>Pin frequently visited feeds</li><li>Smart fetching: Twine looks for feeds when given any website homepage</li><li>Reading view with shortcut to fetch full article</li><li>Bookmark posts to read later</li><li>Search posts</li><li>Background sync</li><li>Feed management: Add, Edit & Pin feeds</li><li>Import and exports your feeds with OPML</li></ul>
