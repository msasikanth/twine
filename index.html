Meet Twine.

A simple & modern feed reader for Android and iOS, with a beautiful reading experience and dynamic theming that adapts to your content.

Download for Desktop

Everything a feed reader should be

Twine supports RDF, RSS, Atom and JSON feeds, and stays out of your way while you read.

Smart feed fetching

Paste any website homepage and Twine finds its feeds for you. Add, edit, pin and group them however you like.

Reader view

Fetch full articles in a clean reader view, and customize fonts and colors to your taste.

Podcast player

A built-in audio player for podcast feeds and HTML audio, right inside your reading flow.

Dynamic theming

The app's ambient color adapts to what you're reading. Light, dark and AMOLED themes included.

Bookmarks & search

Save posts to read later and search across everything. Filter out noise with blocked words.

Widgets & OPML

Home screen widgets, background sync, app icon customization, and OPML import/export.
